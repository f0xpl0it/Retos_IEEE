=== BELL FINGERPRINT ===

Category: Quantum / Cryptography
Difficulty: Easy | 100 pts

A quantum system transmits 8 entangled qubits.
Each qubit was prepared in one of the 4 Bell states.
The channel introduces noise < 5%.

Two measurement files are attached:
  zbasis_measurements.txt  — computational basis
  xbasis_measurements.txt  — dual basis

Identify which state was used in each transmission.

Note: Not all states are distinguishable from a single basis.

Format: IEEE{XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX}
         (16 bits, 2 bits per transmission, order 0→7)
         Φ⁺=00  Φ⁻=01  Ψ⁺=10  Ψ⁻=11
