=== QKD INTERCEPT ===

Category: Quantum / Cryptography
Difficulty: Medium | 300 pts

A BB84 session was recorded in its entirety.
Alice and Bob reached a key agreement.
But something was wrong with the channel.

The log includes all rounds—
those discarded due to incompatible bases and those that counted.

Someone was eavesdropping.
Not all the time. Just at certain moments.
The quantum channel doesn’t lie if you know where to look.

Attachment: bb84_session.csv

Format: IEEE{start1_start2_start3}
         (start indices of compromised windows,
          in sifted rounds, window = 20, QBER threshold = 11%)
