=== TELEPORT TAMPERED ===

Category: Quantum / Cryptography
Difficulty: Medium | 350 pts

Alice prepared a secret quantum state.
She attempted to teleport it to Bob using the standard protocol.

The quantum channel worked perfectly.
The Bell pair was prepared without errors.

But the classical channel was intercepted.
Exactly one of the two correction bits was altered.

Bob applied his correction using the bits he received
and obtained the following state:

  Classical bits received : 10
  Bob's state             : |MINUS⟩

Recover the original state that Alice tried to teleport.
Which qubit did Alice want Bob to have?

Format: IEEE{NAME}
Valid names: ZERO, ONE, PLUS, MINUS, IPLUS, IMINUS
